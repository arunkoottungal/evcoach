How it works

1

Connects to your EC40

Reads speed, battery level, power draw, and ADAS sensor data directly from the car via Android Automotive OS.

2

Fetches conditions ahead

Elevation profile for the next 2 miles, wind speed and direction, precipitation, and variable speed limits.

3

Calculates the optimal speed

Balances energy efficiency against journey time — finds the speed where slowing down saves meaningful energy without wasting significant time.

4

Coaches when it matters

Audio cues only when the difference is meaningful and the road ahead is clear. Never coaches in traffic, near junctions, or in poor conditions.

5

Analyses every trip

Post-trip breakdown by battery zone, route section, and conditions. Shows exactly where efficiency was gained or lost.
