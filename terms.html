EVCoach

EV Efficiency Coaching for Android Automotive

Terms of Use & Privacy Policy
Plain English summary: EVCoach reads data from your Volvo EC40 to coach you on efficient driving. Your trip data stays on your device. We don't sell your data, share it with advertisers, or store it on external servers.

1. About EVCoach

EVCoach is a personal efficiency coaching application for electric vehicles, developed as an independent project. It connects to your Volvo EC40 via the Android Automotive OS platform and the Volvo Connected Vehicle API to provide real-time driving guidance and post-trip efficiency analysis.

EVCoach is currently in an early access / proof-of-concept phase, available to a limited number of invited users.

2. Data We Collect

EVCoach collects vehicle telemetry data for the sole purpose of providing efficiency coaching. The following data is collected during each trip:

Data type Purpose Where stored
Vehicle speed Real-time coaching calculations Device only
Battery level (Wh and %) State of charge analysis, range estimation Device only
Instantaneous power draw (kW) Efficiency model calculation Device only
Estimated range remaining Range anxiety coaching Device only
Odometer reading Trip distance calculation Device only
GPS position and heading Route analysis, elevation and wind data fetch Device only
Trip metadata (origin, destination, time) Route comparison and efficiency trending Device only

We do not collect: personal identity information, payment details, contact lists, camera or microphone data, or any data unrelated to vehicle efficiency.

3. How We Use Your Data

Data collected by EVCoach is used exclusively to:

  • Calculate real-time speed recommendations based on terrain, wind, and battery state
  • Build a personalised efficiency model for your specific vehicle and regular routes
  • Generate post-trip analysis showing where efficiency was gained or lost
  • Track efficiency improvement over time on familiar routes

Your data is never used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond the efficiency coaching features described above.

4. Data Storage and Retention

All trip data is stored locally on your device and, if you use the companion analysis tools, on your personal computer. No trip data is transmitted to or stored on EVCoach servers.

Trip logs are retained on your device until you choose to delete them. You can delete all stored data at any time through the app settings or by uninstalling the application.

Weather data (wind speed, precipitation) is fetched from Open-Meteo, a third-party weather service. Open-Meteo receives only GPS coordinates to return local weather — no personal information is transmitted. Open-Meteo's privacy policy is available at open-meteo.com/en/terms.

5. Volvo Connected Vehicle API

EVCoach uses the Volvo Connected Vehicle API to retrieve pre-trip vehicle state information (battery level, estimated range). This requires you to authenticate with your Volvo ID.

EVCoach requests read-only access to your vehicle data. It does not send any commands to your vehicle and cannot control any vehicle functions.

Your Volvo ID credentials are handled securely via the OAuth2 protocol. EVCoach does not store your Volvo ID password. You can revoke EVCoach's access at any time through your Volvo account settings at volvoid.eu.volvocars.com.

6. Third-Party Sharing

EVCoach does not sell, rent, or share your personal data or vehicle data with any third parties for commercial purposes.

The only external services EVCoach communicates with are:

  • Volvo Connected Vehicle API — to retrieve vehicle state at trip start (read-only)
  • Open-Meteo — to retrieve weather data (GPS coordinates only, no personal data)

7. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to how your data is used

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can exercise most of these rights directly by accessing or deleting the data through the app or your device settings. For any other requests, contact us using the details below.

8. Safety Disclaimer
